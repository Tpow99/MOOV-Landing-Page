What's the MOOV?

MILESTONES

Minimally Viable Product

August 2020

At this stage, we have just completed a bare prototype of the app and have launched it onto our phones. The current working version allows users to post events and find ones that others have posted on the app using Google's Firebase DB and its Flutter engine for mobile devices. We hope to pitch to investors soon.

Pitching to Investors

September 2020

We pitched to an early investor with inside relations and successfully gathered strong interest. We received an $800,000 valuation and an initial seed investment of $20,000 for 2.5% equity. Furthermore, the Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati law firm in Palo Alto has offered to sign us as an LLC with deferred payment until revenue is realized. With this initial investment, we plan to focus on user testing, development, and marketing.

Pre-Accelerator Success

October 2020

We managed to get into the University of Notre Dame's Pre-Accelerator program for early startups with traction. This gives us access to over $100k in resources and $1000 of funding. This 6 week program should put us in front of many more investors, from angel investors to venture capitalists. The startup coaches will speed us through the user testing and marketing process to ensure we have strong guidance as we prepare to launch with the end of COVID.

Demo Day

November 2020

On Demo Day, we will be pitching to multiple potential investors with data proving our user traction and interest in our fully working product. Despite investments, the connections we make on Demo Day will propel us onto our next step for the MOOV product. Our next goal is to engage with local businesses and recruit college ambassadors in each of the dorms on campus to prepare for launch day in the spring.

Our Mission

"We strive to use technology as a means of bringing college students together face-to-face. We hope to democratize the fun on college campuses across the country. In a world dominated by virtual interactions, we hope to pull people out of their comfort zones and explore what's out there. We want to make it easier to bring people together regardless of their background. Let's be the first wave in changing the way people make their MOOV. Let's streamline the move making process and elevate it onto a social scale in a way that's never been done before. Let's start a MOOVment."
